Author Bio

I’m N.F. Nelson, a Canada-based author and illustrator. Through NelsonsWorld Studios, I create original illustrated books ranging from dark mythology-inspired fiction to faith-based graphic storytelling for young readers.

My work blends atmosphere, meaning, and strong visual craft—building worlds that invite readers to explore, reflect, and imagine.

Books

Adult • Dark Mythology

  • Wrath of the Gods
    Dark Mythology • Novel
    Wrath of the Gods: A Tale of Choice and Consequence is Book 1 in the series (a dark, modern mythological series), that explores the return of ancient gods into a fractured contemporary world. As humanity grapples with power, belief, and control, the story follows characters caught between divine forces and human consequence. The novel blends epic fantasy with philosophical and political undertones, examining how myths evolve — and how easily reverence can become manipulation.
